This paper, coauthored with Michael Haan and Taylor Paul at Western University, is published on Population Research and Policy Review. There is a growing body of literature examining both apprenticeship training and journeypersons, because of the ability of these programs to facilitate and maintain a skilled workforce. However, there remains a gap in the literature on the internal migration patterns of these journeypersons from a life course perspective.
